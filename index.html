About

I am a PhD-trained scientist with expertise in pharmaceutical biology, extracellular vesicles, molecular biology, and translational research.

Alongside my scientific background, I have developed practical experience in data analytics, business intelligence, workflow automation, and data engineering. My projects span healthcare analytics, ETL pipelines, dashboard development, and data visualization.

I am interested in roles at the intersection of life sciences, digital transformation, business analysis, and data-driven decision making, where scientific expertise can be combined with analytical and technical skills to create measurable business value.

Life Sciences & Research Projects

Extracellular Vesicles & Immunomodulation

Investigated how bacterial extracellular vesicles influence host immune pathways through isolation, characterization, and functional analysis.

Techniques: EV Isolation, Cell-Based Assays, Gene Expression Analysis, Immunology

Advanced Endothelial Cell Culture Models

Established endothelial cell culture systems using laminar-flow technologies to mimic vascular conditions and study extracellular vesicle production.

Techniques: Cell Culture, Flow Systems, Experimental Design, EV Characterization

Multi-Omics Analysis of Extracellular Vesicles

Integrated transcriptomic and proteomic approaches to characterize extracellular vesicle cargo and identify molecular signatures linked to disease pathways.

Techniques: RNA Sequencing, Proteomics, Bioinformatics, Statistical Analysis

Designed and evaluated multifunctional gold nanoparticle systems for targeted delivery of therapeutic nucleic acids in breast cancer models.

Techniques: Nanotechnology, Molecular Biology, Cancer Research, Experimental Design
